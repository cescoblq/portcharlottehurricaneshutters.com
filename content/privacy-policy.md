Port Charlotte Hurricane Shutters ("we," "us," "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect through this website and how we use it.

**What information we collect.** When you fill out our request form, we collect: ZIP code, project type, and email address (step 1), and — if you continue — shutter details, homeowner status, timeline, first and last name, and phone number (step 2).

**Consent to be contacted (TCPA).** By checking the consent box on our form and submitting your request, you expressly consent to be contacted by phone, text message (SMS), or email about your project — including via automated dialing technology or pre-recorded messages — by Port Charlotte Hurricane Shutters and its partner installers. This consent is not required as a condition of any purchase, and message/data rates may apply to SMS communications. You may revoke this consent at any time by contacting the installer(s) directly or by emailing us at the address on our [contact page](/contact/).

**Consent verification.** To document that consent was given clearly and voluntarily, we use a third-party consent-certification service (TrustedForm by ActiveProspect) that records a timestamped snapshot of the form at the moment of submission. This certificate is shared only with the installer(s) who receive your request, as proof of compliant consent — it does not track your activity elsewhere on the web.

**What we use your information for.** Solely to match your request with licensed local hurricane shutter installers serving your area and to allow them to follow up on your quote request.

**Who we share your information with.** Only with the installer(s) who prepare your quote (typically a small number of installers matched to your project and location). We do not sell your information to unrelated third parties for other purposes.

**How long we keep your information.** We retain request data only as long as necessary to fulfill your request and for a reasonable period afterward for record-keeping and compliance purposes.

**Your rights.** You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your information, and you may opt out of further contact at any time, by contacting us via our [contact page](/contact/). Opt-out requests are processed within 10 business days.

**Security.** Your information is transmitted securely and is protected by anti-bot verification (Cloudflare Turnstile) on our request form.

This policy may be updated from time to time; the version in effect at the time you submit a request governs how that request is handled.
