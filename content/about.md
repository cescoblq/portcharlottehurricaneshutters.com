Port Charlotte Hurricane Shutters is an independent referral service for homeowners in Port Charlotte, FL and the surrounding Charlotte County area who are looking into hurricane shutters.

**We are not a shutter manufacturer or installer.** We don't perform any installation work ourselves — we connect homeowners, free of charge, with licensed local installers who sell and install hurricane shutters in the Port Charlotte area.

**How it works.** You submit a short request through our form describing your project. We match that request with installers who work in your area, and they contact you directly — usually within 24 hours — to schedule a free, no-obligation estimate. You're never obligated to hire anyone through this service.

**How we're funded.** The service is free for homeowners. We're compensated by the participating installers when we send them a qualified request. That arrangement has no effect on the price you're quoted for your project.

**Your information.** We share your details only with the installers who prepare your quote — never with unrelated third parties for other purposes. See our [privacy policy](/privacy-policy/) for the full details, including how we use consent and communication technology like TCPA-compliant contact consent.

Questions about the service? [Contact us](/contact/).
